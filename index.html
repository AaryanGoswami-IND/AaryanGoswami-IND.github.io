01 / About

Crafting Worlds,
One Polygon at a Time

I'm Aaryan — a passionate Game Developer and 3D Artist from the serene hills of Kangra, Himachal Pradesh. I merge technical game development with high-fidelity 3D artistry to create immersive digital experiences.

With hands-on experience in Unity and Unreal Engine development, combined with strong sculpting, modeling, and texturing skills using ZBrush, Maya, Blender, and Substance Painter, I bring game concepts from ideation to polished reality. From precision platformers to detailed character sculpts and cinematic environment art — I thrive at the intersection of art and technology.

Currently pursuing B.Sc. in Animation, VFX & Gaming at Chandigarh University, I'm driven by a relentless curiosity for real-time rendering, procedural workflows, and interactive storytelling.
